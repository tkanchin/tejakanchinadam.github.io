Teja Kanchinadam

2017 - Current
I am a Data Scientist (Machine Learning Research) at American Family Insurance, where I tech lead the building of next generation Human-in-the-loop Machine Learning Systems. This includes dataset gathering, training machine learning models, the science of making it work, and their deployment in production running on cloud infrastructure. Our models quickly learn (real-time) from various forms of human feedback via active learning and state-of-the-art natural language processing (NLP) techniques. The net result is a system that can ingest free-form text and update models successfully without much involvement from data scientists.Publication@KDD20, Publication@IAAI21



I was also responsible for building a pipeline to Predict Customer Satisfaction, which takes customer calls in the form of speech-to-text transcriptions and applies machine learning models. The model is a novel Graph Neural Network algorithm which belongs to the family of non-IID learning and is trained to rank calls. The system has enabled the enterprise to address customer satisfaction 100% of time and thus replaced the previous usage of third party surveys to conduct customer satisfaction which are expensive and limited to only 1% of total calls received. Publication@IAAI21

I was also responsible for the research and development of a novel machine learning algorithm which resulted in the building of an agent Recommender System, thus enabling agents to recommend best product offerings to the next best actions (customer engagement, discounts, etc.). The proposed algorithm reduces the time for structure learning (also inference) on a Bayesian Network (5 days to 25 minutes). The outcome led to 5% increase in total premium accounting to several millions of dollars and 8% increase in policies for new business. Publication@ICMLA18
